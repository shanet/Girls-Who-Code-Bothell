If this is your first time at a club meeting, then this is the page for you. There are a few housekeeping items we ask you to complete before getting started on the coding.

Step 0

Fill out the online student enrollment form

  • For the Club Name, enter "Bothell Library".
  • Uncheck the forms and consent box at the bottom if desired. You'll fill this out on paper.

Step 1

Create a canvas account!

Steps once you get into canvas:

  1. Click on modules
  2. Take the club pre-test
  3. Take the club pre-survey

Useful codes:

  • The club ID number is C-000003655
  • The join code for canvas is CYBCF4

Step 2

Sign up for important club updates by either:
  • Sending a text message to 81010 with the message @f3973b
  • Going to https://www.remind.com/join/f3973b

In the event of a cancellation or other important update, we'll send out a text message to all club members and their parents, but only if you sign up for them!

That's it!

Join in with the rest of the club or ask an instructor for what to do next.
